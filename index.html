What Is Real Estate?
Real estate is both the land and permanent structures which include residences and any enhancements attached to the land. Investing in real estate offers advantages such as generating passive income, maintaining a stable cash flow, enjoying tax benefits, achieving diversification and utilizing leverage.

Trends in Property Market (2010-2028): Fluctuations in Pricing, Listings and Quality
The analysis of property trends from 2010 to 2026 reveals an inversely propotional pattern between average property prices and the number of listings. From 2024 to 2028, a noteworthy shift occurs as the forecasted average price will increase and number of listings will decrease. This shift can be due to a potential reduction in property construction, resulting in decreased market availability. Furthermore, the rising property prices during this period may be indicative of improvements in property quality which attract buyers to invest in higher-priced, higher-quality homes.
