What Is Real Estate?
Real estate is both the land and permanent structures which include residences and any enhancements attached to the land. It is a type of real property which differs from personal property that is not attached to the land. Personal property includes vehicles, boats, jewelry, and more.

Average Property Price of a House and Number of Listings Across Property Construction Years

The analysis of property trends from 2010 to 2023 reveals decrease and cyclical patterns in average property prices and a simultaneous increase in the number of listings. However, from 2024 to 2028, a noteworthy shift occurs as average property prices begin to rise, while the number of listings declines. This shift can be due to a potential reduction in property construction, resulting in decreased market availability. Furthermore, the rising property prices during this period may be indicative of improvements in property quality which attract buyers to invest in higher-priced, higher-quality homes. These findings underscore the dynamic nature of the property market, marked by fluctuations in both pricing and availability over the years.
